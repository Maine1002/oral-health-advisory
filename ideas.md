# Oral Health Advisory — Design Brainstorm

## Approach 1: Editorial Authority (Scientific Journal Meets Consumer Magazine)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets modern health journalism (think The Lancet × Wirecutter)

**Core Principles:**
- Authoritative restraint — every element earns its place
- Information hierarchy through typographic scale, not colour
- Evidence-first: data tables, citations, and methodology are first-class citizens
- Neutral credibility: the site feels like a trusted editorial source, not a brand

**Color Philosophy:**
- Deep navy (#0D2240) as the primary anchor — conveys medical authority and trust
- Warm off-white (#F7F5F0) backgrounds — approachable, not clinical
- Accent: muted teal (#2A7F7F) — health, calm, precision
- Warning/highlight: amber (#E8A020) — used sparingly for "Best Pick" badges

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric editorial grid: left-weighted text columns with right-side data panels
- Long-form article layout with sticky sidebar navigation
- Comparison tables as centrepieces, not afterthoughts
- Full-bleed section dividers using typographic rules (horizontal lines)

**Signature Elements:**
- Large-scale pull quotes with oversized quotation marks
- "Best Pick" ribbon badges in amber on product cards
- Numbered methodology steps with vertical connecting lines

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Minimal animation — content scrolls reveal with subtle fade-in
- Comparison table rows highlight on hover
- Quiz progresses with clean slide transitions

**Animation:**
- Entrance: fade-up (20px, 400ms ease-out) for content blocks
- Table rows: background tint on hover (150ms)
- CTA buttons: subtle scale(1.02) on hover

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif) — authority, editorial weight
- Body: Source Serif 4 — readable, academic
- UI/Labels: DM Sans — clean, modern contrast
</text>

## Approach 2: Clinical Minimalism (Scandinavian Health Tech)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Scandinavian minimalism meets health-tech SaaS (think Headspace × Healthline)

**Core Principles:**
- Radical clarity — one message per screen section
- Generous whitespace as a trust signal
- Data visualisation as a primary communication tool
- Warm clinical: sterile but not cold

**Color Philosophy:**
- Pure white (#FFFFFF) backgrounds — clinical cleanliness
- Sage green (#4A7C59) primary — natural, health-oriented, non-pharmaceutical
- Light mint (#E8F5EE) section backgrounds — gentle differentiation
- Charcoal (#2C2C2C) for body text — softer than black, more readable

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Centered content with generous max-width constraints
- Card-based modular grid for product comparisons
- Sticky top navigation with category mega-menu
- Hero sections with large photography and minimal text overlay

**Signature Elements:**
- Circular progress indicators for product scores
- Thin-line icon set throughout
- Floating "Our Top Pick" cards with subtle drop shadows

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth scroll with section snap points
- Cards lift on hover with shadow transition
- Quiz uses large tap-target radio buttons

**Animation:**
- Section reveals: slide-up (30px, 500ms cubic-bezier)
- Cards: translateY(-4px) + shadow deepening on hover
- Progress bars: animated fill on scroll-into-view

**Typography System:**
- Display: Fraunces (variable serif) — warm, distinctive
- Body: Plus Jakarta Sans — modern, highly legible
- Data/Numbers: JetBrains Mono — technical precision
</text>

## Approach 3: Confident Authority (Premium Health Media Brand) ← SELECTED
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Premium health media editorial — think Wirecutter meets a premium dental journal, with bold typographic confidence

**Core Principles:**
- Bold typographic hierarchy communicates expertise without clinical coldness
- Asymmetric layouts break the "generic health site" mould
- Trust signals are woven into the visual language, not bolted on
- Conversion elements feel like helpful recommendations, not sales pressure

**Color Philosophy:**
- Deep forest green (#1B4332) as the primary brand colour — health, nature, authority (not the typical blue)
- Warm cream (#FAFAF7) as the page background — premium, editorial
- Accent gold (#C9A84C) — "Best Pick" badges, highlights, premium feel
- Soft sage (#A8C5A0) — secondary accents, section backgrounds
- Near-black (#1A1A1A) for headings — maximum contrast and weight

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Offset hero: large headline left, hero image right (not centered)
- Editorial-style article pages with left-rail sticky TOC
- Comparison tables with frozen first column on mobile scroll
- Quiz as a full-screen stepped experience

**Signature Elements:**
- "Editor's Choice" ribbon in forest green + gold
- Thick left-border blockquotes in sage green
- Horizontal rule section dividers with small diamond ornament

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Confident, purposeful micro-interactions
- Comparison rows highlight with green tint on hover
- CTA buttons use forest green with cream text — premium, not aggressive

**Animation:**
- Hero text: staggered word reveal (60ms delay per word)
- Section entrance: fade-up (24px, 450ms ease-out)
- Comparison table: row highlight 200ms ease
- Quiz: slide-left/right transitions between questions

**Typography System:**
- Display: Lora (serif) — editorial authority, warmth
- Body: Nunito Sans — highly legible, friendly, not corporate
- Accent/Labels: Barlow Condensed — bold, efficient, modern contrast
</text>
<probability>0.09</probability>
</response>

## Selected Design: Approach 3 — Confident Authority

Deep forest green + warm cream + gold accents. Lora display serif + Nunito Sans body. Asymmetric editorial layouts. Premium health media feel.
